Currency

Egyptian’s official currency is Egyptian pounds abbreviated by (EGP).
One dollar here in the US is 15.71 egyptian pounds.
The Egyptian pounds symbol is E£. This is how to spell egyptian pound in Egyptian Arabic : جنيه مصرى .
Most people abbreviated the Egyptian pound as LE or L.E.
